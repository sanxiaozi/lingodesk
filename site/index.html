How it works

01 · Receive

They message your personal account

Spanish, Russian, Arabic — any language. LingoDesk picks it up silently; the other side never notices.

02 · Translate

Arrives in your language

Every contact gets a dedicated topic thread. Original and translation side by side — conversations never get mixed up.

03 · Reply

You just type in your language

AI translates it for them. Nothing goes out until you approve the preview — no accidental sends.

04 · Deliver

Sent as you

They see your own account replying fluently in their native tongue.

Built for cross-border conversations

Your real account, not a bot

Built on the official Telegram Business channel. Customers always talk to "you" — trust stays intact.

Language auto-detected & locked

Their language is detected from the first message; every later reply targets it automatically. One command to correct it manually.

Preview before every send

Each translation is shown to you first and sent only on your confirmation. Not sure about the wording? Rephrase and retranslate.

Bilingual archive of everything

Messages, photos and files are archived in both directions — original next to translation, searchable any time.

Ready? Running in 15 minutes

From creating the bot to your first cross-language reply — every step illustrated, every common pitfall flagged. Just follow along.
