About LingoDesk

Chat and do business with the world — in your native language.

What we build

LingoDesk turns your personal Telegram into a cross-language desk. Customers write to you in any language; every message lands translated into yours, in a tidy per-contact thread. You reply in your own language — it's translated back and sent as you, automatically. The other side never sees a bot; they just see you, fluent in their tongue.

It's built for freelancers, cross-border sellers and small teams who close deals in chat — people for whom a missed or clumsy reply is a lost customer.

What makes it different

  • Your own bot, your token. Everything runs through a bot you create and can revoke any time — full control, no black box.
  • Open source, no lock-in. MIT-licensed. Let us host it, or run it on your own server — same code, your choice.
  • Small and focused. An independent product built by people who care about cross-border communication, not growth-at-all-costs.
